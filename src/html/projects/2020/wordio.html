Besta.domains beta Natural Language Processing (text generator) AI APIs. Started out as a quick evening side project, grew into a very robust industry product and consumer app. It's now the best domain suggestions tool in the world. Documentation: besta.domains/api. Get the API: rapidapi.com

Wordio.co alpha Linguistic APIs for AI, writing, or search applications. Properietary database of 300,000 words. Returns root-word, parts-of-speech, sentiment analysis, abbreviations, contractions, singular/plural, etc. Get synonyms (related words), organized by sentiment. Word-breaking, tokenization, lemmatization and spellcheck on text inputs without spaces (useful for domain names).
