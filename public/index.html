2008 - UserFriendlyCMS
Before Wordpress re-designed their UI in 2009, it was difficult to use, though not as difficult as Joomla/Droopal/Typo3. Before the age of Squarespace and Shopify. It was an annoying time for independent website owners who didn't have the time to invest to learn a full featured CMS. I designed and developed this "easy" CMS for my clients. Made with PHP/MySQL on the backend and jQuery UI on the front. It is built into the site itself. It uses custom html tags that lay dormant until the user signs in.
